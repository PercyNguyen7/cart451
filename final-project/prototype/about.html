The Human Algorithm

About

This project aims to pitch human intelligence against AI algorithms in the context of popular social network service. In doing so, one might decipher and understand the methods by which AI functions and the intention of its human designers.

The project, in the form of a website, will showcase a record of my interaction with instagram on three given day. My interactions will be video recorded and displayed on the Homepage. At the end of the virtual exhibition of each Day, participants will be asked to follow the instruction for the given day in the Suggest tab.
